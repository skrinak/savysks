Statement

 

This landscape painting was a practice in using oil. I am familiar with acrylic painting but oil was a new challenge for me. I was aiming to create a landscape with a moody atmosphere.

About Me

 

I am currently a student at Loyola Marymount University. I am studying studio arts and graduate December 2022. I love experiementing with defferent mediums but I mainly focus on drawing and painting. I focus on figurative art in both my drawing and paintings.
